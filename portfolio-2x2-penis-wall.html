2X2 PENIS WALL

A kinetic sculpture that visualizes fluctuations in the stock market.
· Installation    · Data Visualization   
 Previous Back to all works Next 

The 2X2 Penis Wall is a kinetic sculpture consisting of 4 3D-printed erectable penises that were designed to visualize fluctuations in the stock market. Each penis can turn red or green according to the market. There are two RGB LEDs inside each penis unit, and each unit could also be controlled by an Android app.

Hardware and software programmed by Deqing Sun.

Dimensions:12 x 12 x 4 in
 30.5 x 30.5 x 10 cm

Year:2015

Medium:4 3D-printed penis modules
 4 motors
 Bluetooth
 Customized circuit and software
 Wood, Acrylic
