Peiqi Su is a new media artist working in the area of interactive digital art. In her recent bodies of works, Peiqi uses technology to push the boundaries of creative expression. Her kinetic interactive installations and sculptures have the ability to change and respond to realtime data and viewers. These works explored a perspective to view nature, humanity and economy with the emphasis on human-object interaction.

Born in China and trained as an interaction and user experience designer Peiqi lives and works in New York. Her work has been featured on The Huffington Post, Newsweek, Comedy Central, The Creators Project, VICE, Gothamist, ArtNet, SIGCHI 2014, Whatever/无论, Apple Daily/苹果日报, Kotaku, New York Magazine and many other publications.
