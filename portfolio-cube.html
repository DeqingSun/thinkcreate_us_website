The Digital Cube

An ultra-compact LED cube that responds to people's touch.
· Interactive Installation   
 Previous Back to all works Next 

The digital cube is an interactive installation. The concept is about men not owning our true self-determination in the digital world and repeating the same actions as we did with the fruit from the tree of knowledge.

The installation is made with self-made LED cubes with high sensitivity sensors and ultra density LEDs. When people touch the cube, the video on the cube will get blurred.

This Digital Apple was an idea of artist Young Bum Kim.

Exhibition:World Maker Faire at New York Hall of Science

Dimensions:5.5 x 5.5 x 5.5 in
 14 x 14 x 14 cm

Time:2018

Medium:High sensitivity sensor
 Ultra compact electronic and mechenical design
 LEDs
